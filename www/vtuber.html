What is vtuber?

A virtual YouTuber (バーチャルユーチューバー, or VTuber) also name as ctuber(chrarcter youtuber)is an online entertainer, typically a YouTuber, who is represented by a digital avatar generated by computer graphics.

The first use of a CG avatar for vlogging purposes on YouTube was by Ami Yamato who began creating such videos in 2011. The term "Virtual Youtuber" was coined in 2016. Kizuna AI had around 200,000 subscribers in December 2016, and passed two million subscribers after 10 months. Her sudden popularity sparked a trend of VTubers, with an estimated 4,000 in 2018.
